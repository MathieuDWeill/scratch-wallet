# Scratch Wallet Launch Copy

## Headline
Scratch Wallet — a lottery ticket that knows when not to play.

## Subheadline
A risk-capped autonomous DeFi micro-wallet for users who want to explore tiny on-chain opportunities without connecting their main wallet.

## Why it matters
Most autonomous trading bots are dangerous because they overtrade, ignore slippage, and ask users to trust them with valuable wallets. Scratch Wallet flips the default: tiny isolated wallet, hard loss limits, scam protection, and auditable decisions.

## Punchline
Most bots try to get rich. Scratch Wallet tries not to die.
